Percival Hill

Description

The Percival Hill is located between Nicholls and Grace near Gundaroo Dr. At the top Percival Hill there are no structures at the top Percival Hill only an amazing view over the inner north of Canberra. Kangaroos are common occurrence on top of hill and the walk up to hill. To access Percival Hill, you will need to go a short easy hike to reach the summit.

Percival Hill is surrounded by bushland with dirt path leading up to the summit. With the Barton Highway roundabout being at base of a hill.

Percival Hill is a very relaxing place because it offers amazing views and peace and quiet while overlooking the busy Barton Highway roundabout.
