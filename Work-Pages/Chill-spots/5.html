Wighton Playground

Description

This chill location is again in a north suburb but this time Casey. This location is near the top of Casey, it has a playground including swing, Seesaw and a small playground structure. On the playground you get wonderful views of Casey and surround suburbs. Accessing the site is extremely easy because there is parking within 15 metres of the playground.

The surrounds of the Wighton Playground are a small grass area around the playground and then houses roughly 10 metres away.

This playground is very relaxing because Casey is a new suburb so there is little traffic, it is also next to bush land quieter. Again, like all these places they offer amazing views Casey and suburbs
