Steadman Place Tree

Description

Our first chill location is Stedmen Place Tree, which is located on the main hill in the middle of the North suburb Nicholls. At said location you can find a wonderfully large tree with amazing view of the inner north of Canberra. Just a small walk down from the tree there is a wonderfully small playground with swings, slides and other playground features.

The surroundings of the tree is mainly grass hill with 30 meters then beyond there are houses below eye level. The houses area also includes places to park your car to easy access

The relaxing factors of Stedmen Place tree are and un-paralleled view of Nicholls, peace and quite in the middle of Nicholls and a calming environment provided by the nature around it.
