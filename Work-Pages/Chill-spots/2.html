Sue Geh Circuit Playground

Description

The second location in your inner North chill tour is Sue Geh Circuit Playground, is also located just a short walk down from the Stedmen Place hill. At Sue Geh Circuit there is a playground with a slide, monkey bars, swings and a playground structure. The playground can be easily accessed by parking your car on the side of the road then walking like 10 metres

The surrounds include a grassy area with numerous small trees and benches scattered around, with a road on 2 out of the four sides. Houses on all sides but not within 10 metres

The Sue Geh Circuit playground chill factors include tranquillity, playground to chill with friends and calming environment from the nature.
