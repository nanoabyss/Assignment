Hill Reserve

Description

This chill hill spot in located in the north suburb of Ngunnawal. On the peak of Hill Reserve there is only a seat. It does again like all the other places on this list offer an amazing view, especially of Gungahlin and the Gungahlin Lakes golf course. To access the summit of the hill like Stedman Place you will have to park your car on the side of street and take a short walk to the top.

The Hill Reserve surrounds include bushland hill and housing roughly 30 metres behind the seating at the summit.

Hill Reserve offers chill vibes through tranquillity, amazing views and just being at one with nature.
