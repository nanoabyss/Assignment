Atlas Cafe

Atlas Café Website was another university project where I created a website for a café in gungahlin. The website main focus was on design which meant I focused mainly on the CSS and HTML. There was no JavaScript in the website. The goal was to introduce design students on how to make a website so that they could make their only portfolio website.

Documentation

For this website the design process was split into two main stages, planning and research and then creating the website.

Research and Planning

The first stage was conducting research on similar business that have an online presence and then critiquing them and finding design elements that I liked, which would be incorporated into my final designs for the website. The next stage was creating low fidelity wireframes that would be used to create higher quality ones down the line. Also, to gain an understanding of if I liked how the pages would be setup and to find things that needed to be altered down the line.
