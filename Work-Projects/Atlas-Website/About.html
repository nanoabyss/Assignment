About

Atlas Café is located at 33 Hibberson Street Gungahlin, which is located within the Marketplace Gungahlin. Which is the Gungahlin region's main shopping centre located within the Gungahlin Town Centre

Atlas café is Gungahlin’s newest coffee offering. Focusing on both local produce and quality ingredients we strive to pour a great coffee with good food and happy friendly service.

We are local people that enjoy good food and good coffee. We are dedicated to providing a high-quality product that showcases the unique produce of Canberra. We are passionate about our profession and think that your time in a café should be an experience not just a way to sustain yourself.

At Atlas Café we sell coffee beans from Two before Ten and Prism Coffee Company. We also sell coffee making appliances, we currently only offer them in the café.
